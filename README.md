# Predictive Maintenance for Autonomous Electric Buses

## Overview
Rapid urbanization has increased the demand for efficient, safe, and sustainable public transportation systems. Autonomous Electric Buses (AEBs) are emerging as an important solution due to their zero-emission operation and improved safety capabilities.

However, AEBs involve complex mechanical, electrical, and electronic components such as batteries, motors, braking systems, and embedded sensors. Traditional maintenance approaches such as reactive and preventive maintenance often lead to unexpected breakdowns, increased downtime, and higher operational costs.

This research explores the use of **Predictive Maintenance (PdM)** supported by **real-time sensor data and machine learning techniques** to analyze component health, detect anomalies, and study early indicators of potential system failures.
